On June 13-15, the greatest minds at the forefront of Smart Community innovation will convene for the 2016 GCTC Expo at the Austin Convention Center in Austin, Texas.

This three day expo will feature three amazing Smart, Connected Community events in one location: The Smart City Innovation Summit, the GCTC Expo and the US Ignite Application Summit. This will be the most comprehensive showcase and accelerator of Smart Community Innovation in the world:

The Smart City Innovation Summit will be co-hosted by the City of Austin and US Ignite. The event will bring together the leaders and members of more than 100 communities to partner with technology companies and service providers. This even links progressive cities with state of the art solutions and best practices.

The GCTC Expo is a collaborative platform enabling local governments, nonprofit organizations, academic institutions, technologists and private corporations to form project teams and develop ground breaking Internet of Things Applications within the Smart City environment.

The US Ignite Application Summit will provide developers, industry, communities, government, foundations and universities working with software defined networks, cloud computing and gigabit networks a forum to partner and showcase advanced networks and the applications that operate on them.

If you are a member of community leadership, an elected official, business executive, investor, technology developer, an engineer or in any way interested or involved in creating the Next Generation of Smart Communities – then you won’t want to miss these three events.

Attendees will see hundreds of Action Clusters that have developed innovative applications to improve the quality of life in their communities. Innovators in these cities are leveraging software defined networks and state of the art technology to benefit areas such as: energy efficiency, transportation, security, water, healthcare, education, emergency services and community planning. Together, these Action Clusters are defining a new-kind of Smart Community. This event will be their opportunity to show the world what they’ve discovered, invented and created.

The Action Clusters consist of representatives from local governments, non-profit organizations, academic and industry. From Baltimore to Chattanooga to Atlanta to Las Vegas to Spain to Nigeria - residents of these cities will display their hard work and results at the expo. Attendees will also have the chance to interact with ultramodern technology including drones, sensors and unmanned vehicles. They will also have the chance to talk with the Action Clusters to discuss the creation and implementation of each project.

In addition to the trade show booths where attendees can interact with the technology, the event will also feature speakers and panelists discussing a wide range of Smart Community topics, including: government operations, mobility, resilience, networks, infrastructure and energy. Each Action Clusters will also be invited to present their project on the main stage.

Among the cities showcasing their work will be Dayton, OH, Fairfax, VA, Portland Oregon, Valencia, Spain, Rabat, Morocco, Abuja City, Nigeria and many others from throughout the world.
