Overview Agenda Speakers

Please join US Ignite and NIST on June 2, 2015 for a Global City Teams Challenge Strategy Workshop. This half-day workshop will provide an opportunity for GCTC teams to shape the next year of Smart City / IoT initiatives.

During the Workshop attendees will:

  • Hear about US government efforts and initiatives designed to spur the deployment of IoT applications in smart, connected communities
  • Join GCTC participant-led discussions of the support needed to best advance deployments of scalable IoT applications in cities
  • Develop recommendations and a “wish list” for the future of the Global City Teams Challenge to be delivered to US Ignite, NIST, and OSTP

Who Should Attend the GCTC Strategy Workshop?

  • Representatives from Smart City / IoT teams who are interested in shaping future Smart City / IoT initiatives
  • Corporations, educational institutions, non-profits with an interest in smart city projects
