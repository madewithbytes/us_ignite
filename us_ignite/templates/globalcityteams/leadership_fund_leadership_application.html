GCTC Leadership Fund

Application for GCTC Leadership Awards


On behalf of the GCTC Partners IBM, Intel and GE, US Ignite will make cash awards to exceptional Action Clusters, enabling selected Action Clusters to advance their project’s objectives and, in so doing, the objectives of the Global City Teams Challenge.

  • Team Leads from GCTC Action Clusters, including private companies, local governments, non-profit organizations, and educational institutions, regardless of their geographic location, are eligible to apply for GCTC Leadership Awards.
  • Applications for the available funding are due April 15th, 2016. Finalists for the GCTC Leadership Awards will be announced by May 15th, 2016. Please note: distribution of the awarded funds will be made after the GCTC Expo.
  • US Ignite will award a single “GCTC Leadership Award” of $20,000 and up to three (3) $10,000 “Honorable Mention” awards to exceptional applicants.

Any teams wishing to be considered for an award must attend and demonstrate at the GCTC Expo, currently scheduled for June.

Eligible applications for GCTC Leadership prizes will be reviewed by a committee and evaluated on the project’s progress deploying replicable, scalable and measurable IoT projects in a Smart City environment. More specifically, the committee will evaluate applications based on the strength of their accepted GCTC project (40%), the demonstrated progress made by the Action Cluster (25%), the manner the Action Cluster proposes to use GCTC Leadership award (25%) and the composition of their GCTC Action Cluster team (10%).

Additionally, applicants must provide the following information:

  • Name of the Action Cluster applying for the award
  • List all organizations participating in the Action Cluster’s work
  • Indicate how your Action Cluster intends to utilize the GCTC Leadership Funds, if awarded (250 word limit)
  • Indicate how your Action Cluster’s objectives would be advanced by the GCTC Leadership Award Funding (250 word limit)
  • Summary of your Action Cluster’s work to date (300 word limit)
