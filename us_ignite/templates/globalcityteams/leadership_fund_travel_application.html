GCTC Leadership Fund

Application for a Travel Award


On behalf of the GCTC Partners IBM, Intel and GE, US Ignite is pleased to award funding to defray the cost of travelling to the GCTC Expo in June 2016.

  • The Leadership Fund’s travel awards are available to private companies, local governments, non-profit organizations, and educational institutions, regardless of their geographic location.
  • The travel awards for individual participants will range from $500 to $5000.
  • Because funds are limited, a fixed stipend will be awarded to travelers, meaning that the reimbursement may not cover all expenses related to traveling to the GCTC Expo.
  • Applications to the available funding are due April 15th, 2016. Travel awards will be announced by April 30th, 2016. Please note: distribution of the awarded funds will be made after the GCTC Expo.

Applicants interested in travel awards must provide the following information:

  • Name of your Action Cluster
  • Name, organization and title of the person(s) traveling to the GCTC Expo
  • Estimated travel expenses related to your Action Cluster’s proposed participation in the GCTC Expo
  • Description of the objectives related to the Action Cluster(s) in which the applicant is participating that will be advanced by the applicant’s in-person participation at the GCTC Expo (250 word limit)
  • Summary of your Action Cluster’s work to date (250 word limit)
  • Summary of the specific contributions that you, the applicant, have made to your Action Cluster’s overall progress (250 word limit)

A committee, composed of representatives from the GCTC Partners, will score applications based on the strength of the GCTC project in which they are a participant (40%), the applicant’s demonstrated commitment to GCTC (30%) and the applicant’s perceived need for the GCTC Leadership Fund’s limited travel support (30%). A preference will be given to individuals traveling internationally to attend the Expo.
