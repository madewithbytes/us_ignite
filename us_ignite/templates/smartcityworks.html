Smart City Works

Smart City Works is partnering with NIST and US Ignite to provide a unique opportunity for qualified Global City Challenge Teams interested in commercializing their innovations to compete for and be accepted into an upcoming cohort class.


If your team is accepted into the accelerator you will receive the following benefits:

  • An intensive 5-month learning, development, and training program
  • Up to $40k investment in your company
  • Access to world class technical expertise
  • Access to industry leading mentors and executives
  • State of the art design tools from Autodesk, IBM, Interdigital, and others
  • Professional Services Support (product development, legal, accounting, etc.)
  • Direct market exposure
  • Opportunities for access to global test beds for testing and scaling
  • Access to investment capital, infrastructure companies and cities



Qualified Teams Will:

  • Need to be a registered company prior to joining a cohort
  • Be willing and able to travel to and participate in person for the duration of the accelerator experience.
  • The next cohort will take place in the Washington, DC and Boston, Massachusetts regions in the Fall of 2016.
  • Have a product, service, or solution that will significantly improve civil infrastructure, its value chain, and livability in cities.
  • Have a product, service, or solution that has a level of maturity that has a high likelihood of leading to commercialization.



For more information on Smart City Work and participation in a cohort, see Smart Works FAQs.

About Smart City Works

Smart City Works is the world’s first business “actuator” and a premier business accelerator for improving livability and resilience in cities. Smart City Works’ unique focus on the built environment aims to dramatically change the way society designs, build and operates civil infrastructure. Smart City Works operates twice-yearly sessions that are designed to propel graduating companies into the marketplace, endowed with proven products, and equipped with the skills to grow and compete for funding and market share. For more information about Smart City Works, please visit: www.smartcityworks.io.
